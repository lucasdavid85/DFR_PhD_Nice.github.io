Project Overview

This page presents bioinformatic analysis and comparative modeling of CHI1, CHI2a (chalcone isomerases), and CHIL (a CHI-like protein with non-enzymatic functions).

Research Objectives

  • Model 3D structures of CHI1, CHI2a, and CHIL using AlphaFold
  • Compare active sites and evaluate catalytic potential
  • Dock chalcone substrates and evaluate binding efficiency
  • Simulate and predict protein-protein interactions (e.g., CHIL-CHI or CHIL-CHS)

Tools and Techniques

  • AlphaFold2 for structure prediction
  • SwissDock or AutoDock for docking studies
  • ClusPro and HADDOCK for interaction modeling
  • PyMOL, Chimera for structural comparisons

Expected Insights

We aim to determine how structural differences contribute to functional specialization and understand CHIL's role in facilitating enzyme complexes within the flavonoid pathway.
